Indoor Farm

This is the ios app that I developed for use with my senior project’s Indoor Automated Farm. The app allows users the ability to view the status of peripherals such as lights, water pumps, water purifier, etc. It also allowed users to set automation settings for lights and had a manual mode that gave users full control over each of the individual farm peripherals.
